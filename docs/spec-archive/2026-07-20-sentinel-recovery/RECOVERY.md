# Sentinel Epic recovery record

The Sentinel Epic planning authority was recovered from the local review snapshot
created on 2026-07-20. The Public branch had retained an operational
next-session prompt and handover, but had dropped the normative PRD, technical
Spec, and acceptance matrix during Public/Private transfer classification.

This directory is the durable Public-safe planning record. Private transfer
inventories, local runtime receipts, absolute paths, private repository
coordinates, and machine-local handoffs are intentionally excluded. Their
absence is not permission to delete or forget the normative planning scope.

The recovered Epic remains open until its 21 scoped backlog obligations,
their original and Sentinel acceptance criteria, evidence, legal decisions,
publication, and final close gates are independently dispositioned. SNT-A is a
completed prerequisite slice, not the Epic close.

The retention regression is tracked in
backlog/items/2026-07-20-spec-retention-on-close.md.
