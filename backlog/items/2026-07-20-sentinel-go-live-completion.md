# Complete the Sentinel Public go-live boundary

## Description

The Public Core retains a bounded set of Sentinel go-live obligations after the
foundation transfer. Completion needs comparative validator evidence, attested
session behavior, publication evidence, human legal approval, and exact-
candidate close gates. Historic sprint specifications remain archive and
provenance records; they are not Public authority for this work.

## Triggering situation

The Public transfer completeness review on 2026-07-20 found no basis for
copying private project or runtime material, but identified the remaining
neutral Public go-live work that must be completed after the foundation branch.

## Affected artifact

The public Sentinel validation and sandbox contracts, main-session attestation,
documentation and language gates, publication procedure, legal admission, and
exact-candidate release evidence.

## Proposal

Cut independently reviewable Public packages that:

- collect A/B evidence for the native-host validator and the generic plugin
  validator against the same public fixtures, candidate, and acceptance rules;
- keep the strong sandbox lane upstream- and PO-gated until its declared
  authority and assurance are available, with no fallback success claim;
- demonstrate main-session attestation and switchback behavior, including
  fail-closed negative evidence for absent, stale, or mismatched attestations;
- close Public documentation and language-canon obligations before release;
- complete two-channel publication, release, and fetch-back evidence for the
  exact candidate without treating one channel as proof of the other;
- require a human legal and licensing decision before publication admission;
  and
- finish with exact-candidate Verify, Security, privacy, and independent Critic
  evidence.

The package must link to, rather than duplicate,
[`2026-07-20-recovery-preview-callback-attestation.md`](./2026-07-20-recovery-preview-callback-attestation.md)
and
[`2026-07-20-evidence-bound-review-retry-economics.md`](./2026-07-20-evidence-bound-review-retry-economics.md).
Those items retain their own priority, ownership, acceptance, and expiry
boundaries.

Private renderer behavior, private policy, project-specific configuration, and
machine or runtime content are explicitly out of scope. Public implementation
may retain neutral extension points and portable interfaces, but no private
identity, path, repository, remote, secret, receipt, or runtime value may be
copied into the Public Core.

## Ownership and expiry

The next Pipeline Elephant owns triage and an accepted go-live completion
package. The triage due date is **2026-08-10**. If no decision is recorded by
**2026-08-17**, this item expires and must be renewed with current Public
evidence before implementation or release admission.
